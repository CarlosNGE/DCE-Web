Epoxy floor coatings

From Luxurious solid color epoxy to highly decorative and costume-made epoxy floors for commercial and residential.

Solid Color Epoxy Flooring System

Solid-color epoxy flooring is the most common epoxy floor system. Traditionally used in industrial facilities thanks to its durability and longevity. Epoxy flooring is gaining increased popularity amongst home and business owners. The high-gloss finish creates a luxurious and timeless finish, that can brighten up even the darkest space. The bright epoxy colors available can transform any space into a clean, modern, and professional-looking space.

Because of their industrial strength, solid epoxy floors are extremely hard-wearing and long-lasting. They are scratch and abrasion-resistant, tire-marking-resistant, non-porous, stain-resistant, pet-friendly, and UV-stable.

They are also incredibly easy to clean and maintain, thanks to their non-porous surface. With a simple regular cleaning schedule, you can rest assured that your new epoxy flooring will last more than a decade, without losing its appearance.

These floors are great for interior use in residential homes as well as in warehouses, auto repair shops, airplane hangars, car dealerships and showrooms, museums, clinics, shops, grocery stores, and industrial facilities.

Epoxy Flake Flooring system

Do you need flooring that is extremely durable, yet decorative?

Because of their industrial strength, solid epoxy floors are extremely hard-wearing and long-lasting. They are scratch and abrasion-resistant, tire-marking-resistant, non-porous, stain-resistant, pet-friendly, and UV-stable.

Epoxy flake flooring is the most durable decorative epoxy floor system on the flooring market. Composed of multiple layers of resin and vinyl flakes, this system creates a durable and safe walk-on surface. Flake flooring is weather and waterproof thanks to the UV stable topcoat.

Thanks to the seamless non-porous finish, flake flooring is stain and moisture-resistant, as well as hot tire pick-up resistant, abrasion, and scratch resistant.

Flake flooring is one of the very few flooring systems that are ideal for use both indoors and outdoors. It is most commonly found in residential garages, driveways, patios, walkways, and pool decks. This durable and decorative finish is ideal also for commercial and residential properties, that experience high foot and vehicle traffic.

Metallic Epoxy Flooring system

Do you wish there was a floor, that could be fully tailored to your vision and interior design?

With metallic epoxy flooring, you can do just that.

Great for creating bespoke floors, the combination of resin and metallic mica pigments with different application techniques can create floors that can resemble marble, granite, wood, water, or even lava flow patterns, for just a fraction of the price.

Metallic epoxy floors come with a high gloss or matte finish, that is ideal for spaces that are dark or rely on artificial light. Their seamless finish makes them easy to clean and maintain, as well as stain, scratch, and abrasion resistant, making them ideal for properties with high foot traffic.

Metallic flooring is extremely durable and long-lasting, making it ideal for use not only in residential homes but also in busy retail spaces, restaurants, bars, showrooms, and more.

Quartz Flooring System

Are you running a commercial facility, manufacturing facility, or industrial facility that requires a durable, hardwearing, moisture-resistant, and easy-to-maintain floor?

Made out of the finest uniformly shaped quartz granules and epoxy resin, the epoxy quartz floor system was created for the most demanding environments, where safety, durability, hygiene, and ease of maintenance are paramount.

Quartz flooring is waterproof, grease and animal fats resistant, scratch, chip, and abrasion resistant, chemical resistant, and power washing resistant. It can also withstand high-temperature shocks without deforming. The anti-slip finish, provides a safe walking surface, even when the floor is wet.

The non-porous, seamless finish makes quartz flooring anti-bacterial, stain resistant, and extremely easy to clean and maintain.
