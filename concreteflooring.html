Concrete polishing

Polished concrete is a type of floor installation where the existing concrete surface is utilized to create a durable and attractive floor. Even though originally polished concrete was used in commercial installation, it is becoming increasingly popular among homeowners, retailers, and commercial owners.

Concrete is the foundation of every warehouse, industrial, commercial, or retail building.

Polished concrete offers high-quality, durable, and moisture-controlled industrial, commercial, and warehouse flooring. It can improve the appearance and workplace efficiency while allowing your employees and customers firm footing.

Polished concrete can range from low stone exposure and gloss level of 100 grit to high stone exposure, high sheen, and a glass-like surface of 3000 diamond grit.
